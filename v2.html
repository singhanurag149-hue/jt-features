Human Avatar AI Interviewer

v1.0 Type: Product Scope Document Status: Approved

1. Executive Summary

The Human Avatar AI Interviewer is a screening automation platform designed to conduct real-time, face-to-face video interviews. Its primary objective is to replace the time-consuming "First Round" phone or Zoom screen typically conducted by human recruiters.

By utilizing hyper-realistic AI avatars, the system engages candidates in natural, two-way conversation, asking playbook-based questions, handling interruptions, and answering candidate queries about the companydelivering a standardized, unbiased, and 24/7 screening capability.

2. Value Proposition

For the Company: Scale your screening capacity infinitely. Replace hours of repetitive introductory calls with consistent, high-quality AI interviews that deliver instant transcripts and feedback.
For the Candidate: Interview on your schedule, not ours. A stress-free, unbiased interview experience that gives you the time and space to tell your story.

3. Detailed Feature Scope

This section maps functional requirements to specific benefits for Recruiters and Candidates.

A. The "Human" Experience

Features designed to make the AI feel like a colleague, not a calculator.

1. Realistic Avatars (9 Personas) FEAT-A1

Scope: 5 Female and 4 Male high-fidelity avatars (American/European).

Recruiter Benefit "Brand Consistency." Choose a persona that best represents corporate image. Eliminates unconscious bias related to mood/fatigue.
Candidate Benefit "Comfort." Interacting with a realistic face is less intimidating than a blank screen or text chatbot.
2. Low Latency (< 2 Seconds) FEAT-A2

Scope: AI responds to voice inputs in under 2 seconds.

Recruiter Benefit "Realism." High-speed processing ensures the interview feels like a genuine conversation.
Candidate Benefit "Flow." The conversation moves naturally, reducing awkward pauses.
3. Interruption Handling FEAT-A3

Scope: AI stops speaking and listens if the candidate interrupts.

Recruiter Benefit "Soft Skill Assessment." Captures how candidates handle conversational flow and turn-taking.
Candidate Benefit "Control." Allows candidates to interject naturally without breaking the system.
4. Custom Avatar Creation FEAT-A4

Scope: Create a manual avatar using a single photo.

Recruiter Benefit "Hyper-Personalization." Have the CEO "personally" screen every candidate.
Candidate Benefit "Connection." Builds stronger immediate affinity with the company.

B. Conversation Intelligence

Features that manage the logic and flow of the interview.

5. Playbook Creation (Dynamic) FEAT-B1

Scope: Scripts adapt based on uploaded resumes or run as a standard list.

Recruiter Benefit "Standardization." Ensures strictly merit-based screening.
Candidate Benefit "Relevance." Questions are tailored to actual experience.
6. Silence Detection (>30s) FEAT-B2

Scope: AI detects silence and prompts the user ("Are you still there?").

Candidate Benefit "Coaching." Helps nervous candidates who might freeze up, gently guiding them back into the flow.
7. Multi-Lingual Understanding FEAT-B3

Scope: Understands non-English inputs but always replies in English.

Recruiter Benefit "Global Reach." Perfect for assessing non-native English speakers' proficiency.
Candidate Benefit "Safety Net." Lowers stress if they slip into their native tongue briefly.

C. Workflow & Operations

Features that handle the logistics of the meeting.

  • Company Knowledge Base: AI answers FAQs (culture, benefits) based on uploaded docs.
  • Meeting Rejoin: 3-minute window to resume if disconnected.
  • Post-Interview Output: Immediate generation of Video + Transcript + Feedback.
  • Mobile Capability: Full interview functionality on mobile devices.

4. Technical Requirements

The following prerequisites are required to ensure user satisfaction (The "Ask").

Requirement Rationale / Why it matters
English Language The AI operates in English; candidates must have conversational proficiency.
No Background Noise "Quiet Environment Required" Ensures the AI hears the candidate perfectly for accurate transcription.
Good Internet Speed "Video-First Experience" Required for the realistic avatar to load and lip-sync correctly.
